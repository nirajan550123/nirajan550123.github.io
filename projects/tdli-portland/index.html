Transit Dependency Likelihood Index – Portland, Oregon

This project uses GIS, ArcPy, and demographic indicators to build a Transit Dependency Likelihood Index (TDLI) and identify unserved high-need transit areas in Portland, Oregon.

Overview

The tool combines demographic variables, z-score standardization, weighted scoring, and transit service areas to highlight where high-transit-dependent neighborhoods lack nearby bus stops.

Key outputs

    >TDLI score for each polygon. >High-TDLI neighborhoods. >Unserved high-TDLI areas outside service buffers. >Unserved street segments in high-need areas.
